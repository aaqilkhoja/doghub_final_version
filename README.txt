PROJECT TITLE: dogHub

PURPOSE OF PROJECT: The purpose of this project was to create an Android Mobile Application
for connecting indivduals who are dog owners. The app allows users to register for an account 
and log on. Users can create their own profiles which may include several attributes, such their name
and their dog's info. Users can search for other users on the platform and interact with them. 

VERSION or DATE: 1.0 Nov 30, 2021

HOW TO START THIS PROJECT: This app in compaibtle with android devices only. This project can be opened up in
Android Studio, and run through an emulator. 

AUTHORS: Aaqil K. | Harjit S. | Effie K. | Shally S.
 
USER INSTRUCTIONS:

1. Download the project files and run through an Android Virtual Device in Android Studio. 
2. Run and device and Launce the application.
3. Register for an Account.
4. Create your profile.
5. Use the bottom menu navigation to navigate to differnt parts of the app.
6. To open the messaging environment, click on "Message icon" on your home profile.
7. To search for other users, click the "Search Bar" at the bottom.
7b. Search users by typing in their name. 
7c. Search users by their dog characteristics. Type in the characteristic and click on "Search by Characteristics".
8. To view the profile of a user in the results, click on "View Profile".  
9. To send a message to the user while on their profile, click on "Send Message". 
10. To view events, click on the "Feed" icon at bottom right. 
11. To post your own event, click on the "plus icon". 
12. To edit your profile, click on the sandwich menu on the top left, and click on Edit Profile. 
13. To delete your profile, click on the sandwich menu on the top left, and click on Delete Profile. 
14. To Log Out, click on the sandwich menu at the top left, and click on Log out.
